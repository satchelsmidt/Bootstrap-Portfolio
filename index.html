About Me

Hello -- welcome to my online portfolio! I am a recent graduate from the University of Washington, with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, focusing in Information Systems and Operations Management. After learning about the many managerial aspects of software development, I decided to take a hands-on approach to development and learn to create websites and applications of my own. Software development is fascinating to me because of the creativity it gives developers, and the aesthetic influence they have over their projects. I am constantly looking to learn new things and grow as a person, which means that I have many passions aside from developing. Cooking, reading, improv comedy, and fitness are just a few of these, and balancing all of them is another full time hobby as well!
